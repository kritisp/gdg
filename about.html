Our Story ↓

Our Mission

To empower every organization with intelligent, secure, and scalable tools that eliminate complexity, unlocking human potential and accelerating innovation.

The Innovato Journey

From a simple idea to a globally-trusted platform, our story is one of persistent innovation.

2021

The Idea

Innovato is born from a single question: "Why is business software still so complicated?" The first lines of code are written.

2022

Nexus AI Launch

Our first product, Nexus AI, launches in beta, gaining 100+ early adopters in just two months.

2023

Series A Funding

$15M in funding secured to expand our security offerings and scale the engineering team.

2024

Shield & Automata

We launch two new products, Shield Security and Automata, creating a true B2B suite.

2025

Global Expansion

Innovato opens its first international office in London and surpasses 2,000 active teams worldwide.

Our Core Values

These aren't just words on a wall. They're the principles that guide every decision we make.

Customer-Obsessed

We build for our customers, not for ourselves. Their success is our success, and their feedback is our roadmap.

Example: Our engineers join support calls weekly to hear directly from users.

Boldly Curious

We challenge the status quo and ask "what if?" Curiosity drives us to find better, faster, and smarter solutions to hard problems.

Example: We host monthly 'Hack Days' where anyone can build and pitch a new idea.

Own with Integrity

We act with transparency and accountability. We do the right thing, especially when it's hard, and we own our outcomes.

Example: We publish our security audits and uptime data publicly.
